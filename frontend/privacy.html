1. Introduction

Welcome to WebShield. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how WebShield ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our web security platform and services.

By using WebShield, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

Company Information:

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information when you register for an account:

  • Account Information: Full name, email address, and password (encrypted)
  • Profile Data: Optional profile information you choose to provide
  • Communication Data: Information you provide when contacting our support team

2.2 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use our services:

  • Scan Data: URLs you scan, scan results, and threat analysis reports
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address, and device identifiers
  • Usage Analytics: Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform, and interaction patterns
  • Log Data: Server logs, error reports, and performance metrics

2.3 Browser Extension Data

If you use our browser extension, we collect:

  • URLs visited for real-time threat detection
  • Extension settings and preferences
  • Threat detection events and user responses

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

3.1 Service Delivery

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our web security services
  • Perform URL scanning and threat analysis
  • Generate security reports and analytics
  • Deliver real-time protection through our browser extension

3.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our platform
  • Develop new features and functionality
  • Enhance threat detection algorithms using machine learning
  • Optimize performance and user experience

3.3 Communication

  • Send service-related notifications and updates
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Provide security alerts and threat notifications
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)

3.4 Security and Compliance

  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and other policies
  • Protect the rights and safety of our users

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • Access Controls: Role-based access control (RBAC) and multi-factor authentication for administrative access
  • Infrastructure Security: Secure cloud infrastructure with regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Password Protection: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and incident response procedures
Security Commitment: We continuously update our security practices to protect against emerging threats. For more details, please visit our Security Policy.

4.2 Data Location

Your data is stored on secure servers located in compliance with applicable data protection regulations. We use reputable cloud service providers with robust security certifications.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to enhance our platform:

5.1 VirusTotal API

We integrate with VirusTotal API for comprehensive threat intelligence and URL scanning. When you scan a URL, it may be sent to VirusTotal for analysis. VirusTotal's privacy policy applies to data processed by their service.

5.2 Analytics Services

We may use analytics services to understand how users interact with our platform. These services collect anonymized usage data to help us improve our services.

5.3 Cloud Infrastructure

Our services are hosted on secure cloud infrastructure providers that comply with industry-standard security certifications including SOC 2, ISO 27001, and GDPR.

Important: We carefully vet all third-party services and ensure they maintain appropriate security and privacy standards. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

6.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for authentication and core functionality
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our platform
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Collect anonymized data about usage patterns

6.2 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may limit your ability to use some features of our platform.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

7.1 Access and Portability

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Export your scan history and reports

7.2 Correction and Deletion

  • Update or correct inaccurate personal information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (where applicable)

7.3 Objection and Restriction

  • Object to certain types of data processing
  • Request restriction of processing in specific circumstances
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
Exercising Your Rights: To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@webshield.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

8.1 Account Data

  • Active Accounts: Retained for the duration of your account
  • Deleted Accounts: Personal data deleted within 90 days of account deletion
  • Backup Data: May persist in backups for up to 180 days

8.2 Scan Data and Logs

  • Scan History: Retained for 12 months or until account deletion
  • System Logs: Retained for 90 days for security and debugging purposes
  • Aggregated Analytics: Anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for research and improvement

8.3 Legal Requirements

We may retain certain data longer if required by law, to resolve disputes, enforce our agreements, or for legitimate business purposes.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and implement appropriate safeguards, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by regulatory authorities
  • Adequacy decisions by relevant data protection authorities
  • Privacy Shield certification (where applicable)

10. Children's Privacy

WebShield is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete such information.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending email notifications for significant changes

Your continued use of our services after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
